# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as members, contributors, and maintainers, pledge to make participation in our project and community a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

We are committed to fostering an open, inclusive, and collaborative environment regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, level of experience, education, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

# Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

* Being respectful and considerate.
* Using welcoming and inclusive language.
* Accepting constructive feedback gracefully.
* Focusing on what is best for the community.
* Showing empathy toward other contributors.
* Helping newcomers learn and grow.
* Respecting different viewpoints and experiences.
* Reporting issues responsibly.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment or abusive language.
* Personal attacks or insults.
* Discrimination of any kind.
* Trolling or inflammatory comments.
* Publishing private information without permission.
* Spam or intentionally disruptive behavior.
* Threats or intimidation.
* Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

---

# Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior.

---

# Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including:

* GitHub Issues
* Pull Requests
* Discussions
* Project documentation
* Community forums
* Other official communication channels related to this project

It also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

---

# Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately to the project maintainer.

When reporting an incident, include:

* A description of what happened.
* Relevant screenshots or logs (if applicable).
* Links to related issues, pull requests, or discussions.
* Any additional information that may help resolve the situation.

Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with appropriate confidentiality.

---

# Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers may take actions including:

### 1. Correction

A private warning explaining why the behavior was inappropriate and requesting improvement.

### 2. Warning

A formal warning for repeated or more serious violations.

### 3. Temporary Restriction

Temporary suspension from participating in the project.

### 4. Permanent Ban

Permanent removal from the community for severe or repeated violations.

---

# Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining a professional, welcoming, and inclusive community where everyone can collaborate respectfully.

Constructive discussions, thoughtful feedback, and teamwork are encouraged.
